Sightseeing

I want to go to this tourist spot in France !

The Palace of Versailles

The palace, built in 1682 by Louis XIV, is famous for being the most luxurious palace in the world. It was also registered as a UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1979. There are many places to admire, not only the interior of the palace like the Hall of Mirrors, but also the Fountain Square, Marie Antoinette's hamlet, and the Grand Trianon Palace.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre, located in Paris, is the oldest museum in Europe. There are many world-famous works such as the Mona Lisa and the Venus de Milo. However, it is impossible to see all the works in one day because the site is so large and there are so many exhibits.

Mont Saint-Michel

Mont Saint-Michel is a small island on the west coast of France and a monastery that rises above it. At high tide, the island is completely surrounded by the sea. It is so beautiful that it is also called the "Wonder of the West". Although there are no brand-name stores, there are many stores where visitors can buy local specialties and souvenirs.
