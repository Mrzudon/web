University of Caen-Normandie

This section summarizes the university information.

University

The university was founded in 1432 and is one of the oldest universities in Europe. It has six campuses in Caen and five campuses in Normandy. There are about 30,000 students. One of its features is that it is international with 264 agreements all over the world.

Carré International

Carré International is a language school affiliated with the university. The classes are divided into seven levels, from beginner to advanced, with small class sizes.
