University of Caen-Normandie

This section summarizes the university information.

University

The university was founded in 1432 and is one of the oldest universities in Europe.

It has six campuses in Caen and five campuses in Normandy.

It is a comprehensive university with about 30,000 students.

It is an international university with agreements with 264 institutions around the world.
